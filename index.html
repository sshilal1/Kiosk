    Solar Energy

    If you head to the room of the Engineering and Science Building, you will find a massive array of solar panels delivering clean and cheap energy to the E&S Building!

    • Binghamton's E&S Building was finished in 2011, and recording of solar energy began a year later in 2012
    • The amount of solar energy that falls on the earth’s surface in 40 minutes equals the total annual energy consumption of all the world’s people
    • Even though Binghamton is cloudy, the system still produces 25% of its normal energy through the clouds
    • To learn more, check out the E&S Solar Panel Website
